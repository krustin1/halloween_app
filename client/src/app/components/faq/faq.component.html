FAQ

It will be a Halloween party with costumes highly encouraged. We will have snacks and drinks. We have some games planned with prizes, including a costume contest.

We will have snacks, but it is BYOB. We will have some beer available.
Feel free to bring snacks as well but please ensure it is vegan. Thank you!

Can I bring/invite more than one other person?

Yes, just please let us know if how many and who you plan to bring!
